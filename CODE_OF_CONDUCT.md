# Code of Conduct

`runmark` aims to be a respectful, constructive, and inclusive open-source project.

## Expected behavior

- be respectful and professional
- assume good intent and discuss disagreements in good faith
- give actionable feedback
- welcome contributors with different backgrounds and experience levels

## Unacceptable behavior

- harassment, intimidation, or personal attacks
- discriminatory language or exclusionary behavior
- doxxing or publishing private information without permission
- bad-faith disruption, trolling, or deliberately hostile conduct

## Scope

This code of conduct applies to project discussions, issues, pull requests, reviews, and other community spaces connected to `runmark`.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, code, issues, and other contributions that violate this policy. Repeated or severe violations may result in temporary or permanent exclusion from the project.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, report it privately to the project maintainers through GitHub rather than escalating in public.
